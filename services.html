Our Services

At Nagpur Drycleaners, we offer a wide range of premium dry cleaning services tailored to your needs. From delicate traditional wear to everyday essentials, our advanced technology and expert care ensure your items shine.

Traditional Wear

We specialize in cleaning delicate traditional garments like kurtas, sarees, sherwanis, and Indo-Western outfits. Our gentle cleaning methods preserve intricate embroidery and delicate fabrics, ensuring your attire stays vibrant, fresh, and perfectly maintained.

Formal Attire

From business suits to tuxedos, we provide expert cleaning for formal wear, maintaining a crisp and polished look. Whether for business meetings or special events, our careful attention to detail ensures your attire remains immaculate, ready for any occasion.

Accessories & More

We clean a wide range of accessories and household items, including shoes, bags, blankets, curtains, and carpets. Our specialized cleaning techniques restore their original look, removing stains and odors while ensuring every detail is handled with care and precision.

Upholstery & Furnishing Cleaning

We offer professional cleaning services for home and car furnishings, such as sofas, armchairs, cushions, throws, pillows, and car seats. Our gentle yet effective cleaning methods eliminate dust, stains, and odors, keeping your furniture and vehicle interiors looking fresh and well-maintained.

Pickup and Delivery

Our convenient pickup and delivery service ensures a hassle-free dry cleaning experience. We collect your items and return them freshly cleaned, saving you time and offering the convenience of high-quality care right at your doorstep.

Express Service

For urgent cleaning needs, our express service offers fast turnaround with quick pickup and drop-off. We maintain high standards even under tight schedules, ensuring your items are cleaned efficiently and promptly without compromising on quality.

Commercial Laundry & Dry Cleaning

We provide top-tier laundry and dry cleaning services for businesses like hotels, restaurants, and corporate offices. From linens to uniforms, we ensure all textiles are cleaned to the highest standards, helping businesses maintain a professional and polished appearance.

Steam Cleaning

Our steam cleaning service uses high-temperature steam to deep clean and sanitize fabrics, upholstery, and carpets. This eco-friendly method removes dirt, stains, and bacteria without chemicals, leaving your items fresh and revitalized. Ideal for delicate fabrics and high-touch areas.

Pet Odor Removal

Our pet odor removal service targets and neutralizes stubborn smells in fabrics like pet beds, clothing, upholstery, and carpets. Using safe, effective techniques, we ensure your items are not only odor-free but also deeply refreshed, providing a cleaner and more comfortable environment for you and your pets.
